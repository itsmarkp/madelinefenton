I use my own hand-prepared materials and apply the same principles as those used by artists of the 15th and 16th centuries. You can see how I work on the techniques page.

The panels are small -- maximum 30 cm (12 inches) square and have an exquisite, jewel-like quality. Tiny brushes allow me to reproduce minute detailing, such as fur and whiskers. 

I have exhibited in Cambridge and London. My work is in private collections in England and the USA.

My training in the techniques of the old masters was learned in the studio of renaissance expert Dr David Cranswick, over a period of several years.
